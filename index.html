I am a software engineer with a passion for creating long term accessibility solutions. I first got into coding tinkering with an arduino in college building out speculative social support devices. Before tech, I worked in direct care with others experiencing disabilities. I know that building with disabilities in mind makes our environment/ products easier and more fun to navigate for everyone. Whether it is an explicit goal, in the job description or not - my passion for universal design has built my attention to detail for clean, efficient code and usable apps.
